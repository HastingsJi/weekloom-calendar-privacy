Privacy Policy

Weekloom Calendar - Your privacy matters to us

Last Updated: July 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Weekloom Calendar ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience with our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Weekloom Calendar iOS application.

By using Weekloom Calendar, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

Weekloom Calendar collects minimal personal information:

  • Birthday Date: We collect your birthday to calculate your life calendar visualization

How We Collect Information

  • You provide your birthday directly through the app's date picker
  • This information is stored locally on your device
  • We do not collect any information automatically

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, or other personal identifiers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Any information from third-party services

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • Life Calendar Visualization: To create your personalized 90-year life calendar
  • Progress Calculations: To calculate your age, weeks lived, and remaining weeks
  • App Functionality: To provide the core features of the app

We do not use your information for:

  • Marketing or advertising purposes
  • Sharing with third parties
  • Analytics or tracking
  • Any commercial purposes

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using secure iOS storage mechanisms:

  • Your birthday is stored using AsyncStorage (iOS secure storage)
  • Data never leaves your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No cloud storage or backup of your personal data

Data Security

  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • Your data is protected by iOS security features
  • No external access to your personal information
  • Data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share your personal information with any third parties.

This includes:

  • No data sharing with advertisers
  • No data sharing with analytics services
  • No data sharing with social media platforms
  • No data sharing with any external services

Your birthday and all app data remain completely private and local to your device.

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • You can view your stored birthday in the app settings
  • You can change your birthday at any time using the "Reset" feature
  • You can delete all data by uninstalling the app

Data Portability

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your information. You can:

  • Access your data through the app
  • Modify your data through the app interface
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app

Children's Privacy

Weekloom Calendar is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

International Users

Weekloom Calendar is designed to comply with privacy laws worldwide, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy regulations

Since we do not collect, transmit, or store data externally, we maintain the highest standards of privacy protection for all users worldwide.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing notice through the app if significant changes occur

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
